Nature Pharmacy Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to NaturePharmacy's Privacy Policy. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our application and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

This policy applies to NaturePharmacy mobile application and related services provided by NatureTech Solutions LLC (referred to as "we", "us", or "our" in this privacy policy).

Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes:

Personal Information:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Date of birth
  • Profile pictures
  • Health information (allergies, medical conditions)
  • Remedy preferences and history

Usage Data:

  • App usage statistics
  • Interaction with features
  • Device information
  • Log data

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our Service
  • Personalize your experience
  • Improve our App
  • Provide customer support
  • Send notifications about updates or changes
  • Generate health insights and recommendations
  • Ensure the safety of recommended remedies
  • Comply with legal obligations

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data based on:

  • Your consent (which you can withdraw at any time)
  • The necessity to perform our contract with you
  • Our legitimate interests in providing and improving the service
  • Compliance with legal obligations

For health-related data, we specifically obtain your explicit consent before collection and processing. You have the right to withdraw this consent at any time through the app settings.

Data Retention Policy

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

Specifically:

  • Account information: Retained while your account is active
  • Health data: Stored for 3 years after your last app use
  • Usage data: Kept for 2 years for analysis purposes
  • Consultation records: Maintained for 5 years due to health record requirements

You can request deletion of your data at any time through your account settings.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security measures. We use:

  • Firebase secure cloud storage with AES-256 encryption for your account data and preferences
  • Local device encryption for sensitive health information that doesn't need to be shared
  • Secure data transmission via TLS/SSL protocols when communicating with our servers
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments of our systems
  • Access controls limiting employee access to user data based on need-to-know principles

Camera Usage

The NaturePharmacy app requests permission to use your device's camera for the following purposes:

  • To capture images of plants for plant identification
  • To scan product bar codes to retrieve product information
  • To upload profile pictures to your account

Your camera is only accessed when you explicitly choose to use a feature that requires it. Images captured are:

  • Processed locally on your device when possible
  • Securely transmitted to our servers when needed for identification or analysis
  • Stored in your account only if you choose to save the image

You can revoke camera permissions at any time through your device settings.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our app (Firebase, AWS)
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Analytics partners (Google Analytics in anonymized form)
  • Healthcare providers (with your explicit consent)

All third-party service providers are contractually obligated to use your data only for the purposes specified by us and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
